Fanshawe College’s Interactive Media Design program is a dynamic and comprehensive three-year diploma program that immerses students in the exciting world of interactive media and digital design. With a strong emphasis on both creative and technical skills, this program equips students with the

knowledge and hands-on experience needed to excel in the rapidly evolving digital media industry.

 

Students delve into the core principles of design, gaining proficiency in graphic design, typography, and user interface (UI) design, while also developing technical competencies in web development, multimedia production, and programming for interactive applications.
